Money in Politics

This website is an attempt to dive into the contributors -- and beneficiaries -- of US federal politics. We have scraped data from the FEC (Federal Election Commission), and analyzed the data with several visualization tools (Sankey charts, geographic and network diagrams) in order to more clearly convey these spending trends to the public.
